Red Velvet develops and publishes mental health apps for mobile devices. Red Velvet or we or us refers to Red Velvet Inc. in this Privacy Policy. In conjunction with the Ministry of Education in Ontario, Red Velvet Inc. operates the app Flikr. 



This Privacy Policy applies whenever you use our applications or any of our products, services and content. This Privacy Policy describes:



         What information we collect, how we collect it, and why

         How we use that information and with whom we share it

         How this information is retained and deleted



If you do not want Red Velvet to collect, store, use, or share your information in the ways described in this Privacy Policy, you may not use Flikr.

   

    1.1 INFORMATION WE COLLECT



Information you provide us directly:



  • Your username, password and email address when your school registers you for a Flikr account.
  • Profile information you provide us for user identification (e.g., first and last name, phone number). This information allows us to help you find suitable mentors on Flikr.
  • User Content (e.g., photos, comments, chats, and other materials) that you post to the service.
  • Communications between you and Flikr. For example, we may send you service-related emails such as account verification, changes/updates to features of the service, technical and security notices. You will not be able to opt out of service-related emails.



Information collected automatically:

  • Information generated by your activity (e.g, usage data, preferred language, date and time, areas of our app that you visited)



Information from other Sources:



  • If we receive any information about you from other sources, we may add it to information we have about you. For example, if your school provides us with information about your current state of mind, we may suggest in-app that you seek additional help through our resources page.



    2.1 HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION



We use your personal information to:



  • Respond to your comments, questions or concerns
  • Notify you on new updates and changes to the app
  • Analyze usages rates to improve our content and effectiveness
  • Provide support to help keep Flikr safe and secure
  • Allow you to personalize your experience to make the app a better experience



Keeping our customers privacy secure is of the utmost importance to us, so we, along with the Ministry of Education in Ontario and the Government of Canada, can guarantee that your information is secure and will not be shared with any other corporation or company.



    3.1 HOW YOUR INFORMATION IS RETAINED AND DELETED

The information listed above will be retained in our database to ensure the safety of all its users. 

The following information will be deleted from our database under the conditions listed below:

  1. All information is deleted if your account is deleted
  2. All user content will be erased from our database after 18 months
  3. Collection of all information generated by your activity can be turned off in settings
